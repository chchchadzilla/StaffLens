## Interview Focus Areas

Tell the AI what topics are important to ask about. Be specific!

**Primary Topics to Explore:**
- Their gaming background and what games they enjoy
- How they handle losing or frustrating situations
- Their experience with team-based activities
- What they're looking for in a gaming community
- How much time they can dedicate to being active

**Personality Traits We Care About:**
- Good sport (doesn't rage or blame others)
- Friendly and approachable
- Willing to learn and teach others
- Shows up when they say they will
- Can take jokes and banter without getting offended

**Red Flags to Watch For:**
- Toxic behavior or excessive negativity
- Blaming others for their mistakes
- Elitist attitude toward newer players
- History of drama in other communities
- Only interested in what they can get, not what they can give

## Interview Style

**Tone:** Casual and friendly - like chatting with a potential new friend

**Special Instructions:**
- Use gaming terminology if they seem familiar with it
- Ask about their favorite gaming moments
- Keep it light and fun, not like a job interview
- If they mention streaming or content creation, ask about that too

## About This File

The AI interviewer reads this file to understand your community and tailor its questions accordingly. 

**Tips for customizing:**
1. Be specific about what makes YOUR community unique
2. List concrete traits you're looking for (not just "be nice")
3. Include topics relevant to your community's focus
4. The AI will naturally weave these into conversation - it won't read them like a checklist

**Examples by community type:**

- **Art Server:** Focus on creative process, giving/receiving critique, collaboration
- **Study Group:** Focus on commitment, helping others, time management
- **Business/Networking:** Focus on professional goals, what they offer, reliability
- **Anime/Fandom:** Focus on engagement, respecting different opinions, event participation
- **Tech/Dev:** Focus on projects, learning attitude, collaboration style
